Our Journey

From humble beginnings to a global community.

1990

Teton Sports was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality outdoor gear at an affordable price.

2000

Launched our first flagship backpack, the Explorer 4000, which quickly became a favorite among hikers.

2010

Expanded our product line to include tents and sleeping bags, offering a complete camping solution.

2018

Reached a milestone of 1 million happy customers worldwide and opened our first international office.

Present

Continuing to innovate with sustainable materials and community-driven designs for the modern adventurer.
