Frequently Asked Questions

What is the warranty on Teton backpacks?

We offer a Lifetime Warranty on all Teton Sports products. If your product fails due to a manufacturing defect, we will repair or replace it free of charge.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on your location. You can calculate shipping at checkout.

How do I clean my backpack?

We recommend hand washing your backpack with mild soap and warm water. Do not use a washing machine or dryer, as this can damage the fabric and coating.

Can I return my product if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. We have a 30-day return policy. If you aren't completely happy with your purchase, you can return it for a full refund within 30 days of receipt.
