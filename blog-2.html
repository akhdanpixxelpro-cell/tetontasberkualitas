Top 5 Hidden Gems to Visit in 2024

Escape the crowds and discover something real.

The breathtaking view of the Albanian Alps.

  • 1. Sumba Island, Indonesia
  • 2. The Albanian Alps
  • 3. Choquequirao, Peru

As popular destinations get overcrowded, the true traveler seeks the unknown. Here are our top picks for 2024 that offer beauty, solitude, and adventure.

1. Sumba Island, Indonesia

Often overshadowed by its neighbor Bali, Sumba offers rugged terrain, ancient culture, and untouched beaches. It's a paradise for surfers and adventurers alike.

Read Also: The Ultimate Guide to Packing Light

2. The Albanian Alps

Europe's last secret. The mountains here rival the Swiss Alps but without the tourists or the price tag. Hiking from Valbona to Theth is a must-do experience.

"The world is big and I want to have a good look at it before it gets dark." – John Muir
Ancient ruins waiting to be explored.

3. Choquequirao, Peru

Known as the "sister" of Machu Picchu, this ruin is only accessible by a two-day hike. The result? You might have the entire ancient city to yourself.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe to travel solo to these places?

Sumba and Albania are generally very safe and welcoming to tourists. Always exercise standard precautions.

What is the best time to visit?

For Albania and Peru, May to September is ideal. Sumba is best during the dry season from May to October.
